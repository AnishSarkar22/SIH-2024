Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get in touch with a mentor?

We offer two main ways to get in touch with a mentor, the regular long-term mentorship through application, and by booking a session.

How much do mentors cost? How does pricing work?

Each mentor offers multiple pricing tiers and has different offers. With the mentorship subscription, you'll get charged the monthly fee of the mentor you're subscribed to. If you book a session, you'll be charged the price of the session once.

What can I expect from mentors?

Mentors are vetted and continuously evaluated based on their performances, with the goal to only have the best mentors available to you. Their goal is to get you closer to your goal with the services booked in your plan.

However, mentors are professionals in the industry, offering their free time to help you reach your goals. You'll typically receive replies within a few hours and will have pre-scheduled meetings with your mentor, but they cannot be available to you 24/7.

My mentor hasn't responded to my application in some days, what should I do?

We're continuously looking after the mentors and their activity. However, at times it can be the case that a mentor is unavailable, even though they were recently active. In that case, if you have not received a reply in more than 5 days, it's a safe bet to reach out to someone else.

Can I withdraw my applications?

If you've changed your mind about a mentor, you can withdraw your application. The withdrawal option is available three days after your application, to allow the mentor to react.

How many mentors can I reach out to?

We impose a light limit on the number of mentors you can reach out to in a given day and week for spam and security reasons. However, there's no limit on the number of mentors you can subscribe to.

Guide Me

A generic mentorship platform that bridges the gap between mentors & mentees catering to the needs of users for their progress, skill-building & personal concerns.​

Automated Calendar Booking System – Allows easy scheduling of mentor-mentee sessions.

Embedded Video Call & Chat Features – Enables virtual meetings with real-time communication.

Personal Guidance & Skill Development – Ensures accessibility, availability, and personalized support for mentees.

Gamification & Reverse Mentoring – Innovative features to engage users and facilitate learning from both mentees and mentors.

Data-Driven Algorithm for Matching – Uses algorithms to intelligently match mentors and mentees based on their needs and profiles.
